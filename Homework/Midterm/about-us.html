Who We Are

June's Bug System is a pest control company that is focus on getting rid of pest as safely as possible.Our headquarters is located near Los Feliz, California. We focus on helping out local residences and businesses located in Los Angeles Country or near by counties. We use natural pesticides for bugs, like roaches, spiders, and bed bugs. Our pesticides are organic and safe for children and pets.All raccoons or possoms are live caught and release into the wild away from civilization. Bees, Wasps, and yellow jackets are neutralize before removing their nest. We try our best to avoid killing all animals.
